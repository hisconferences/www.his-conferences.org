Conference Venue

The HIS2023 conference will be held at the City Campus of Victoria University, Melbourne, Australia. The campus is located in the heart of Melbourne CBD and is easily accessible by public transport. A wide range of accommodation options, restaurants, cafes, and tourist attractions surrounds the campus.

Address: Rooms ROGL02 and RO0102, VU City Queen Campus, 295 Queen Street, Melbourne, Victoria
