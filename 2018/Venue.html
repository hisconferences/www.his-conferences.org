Conference location:

HIS 2018 will take place at James Cook University, Cairns Campus, and Cairns, Australia
Building: D3 Cairns Institute.

 

Map External Link

Room: D3-054 Tiered Theatre.

1/14-88 McGregor Rd, Smithfield QLD 4878.
The campus location is spectacular – surrounded on three sides by rainforest-covered mountains. Located near the northern beaches in the suburb of Smithfield, the campus is 15 kilometres (9 miles) north of the city centre.

Transportation from the city centre in Cairns to the conference location
(1) bus
To travel to the conference location on JCU Campus located in Smithfield, you can take the Sunbus Bus Service. 

JCU campus is located a 30 minute express bus ride from the city centre.
The buses depart from Cairns City (from the Cairns Central Shopping Centre) regularly.
