Registration Fees

HIS 2021 registration rate (the registration deadline is 10th, September 2021):

Registration fee
Author registration (for 1st paper) AUD 400
Author registration (from 2nd paper on) AUD 300

To register, please either register online or make a bank transfer:

  • Register online using VU secure payment system
  • Bank transfer to bank account (the transfer description should be "HIS Conf 21 and your name")

Additionally, you are advised to fill in the Registration Form (word, pdf), sign it, and email it to Hua.Wang@vu.edu.au with the subject Participant’s Registration (format of the registration attachment name: conference title + paper ID + name). Please note we are not allowed to have your credit card details, so you do not need to provide your credit card details in the form.

Policies

Author Registration

Author registration includes admission to all HIS 2021 conference and workshop sessions, and electronic conference proceedings.

For Paper Authors

  • Authors of an accepted paper must complete a full registration by 10th September 2021 for their papers to be included in the conference proceedings.
  • If you have multiple accepted papers, the first paper needs a full author registration. From the second paper on, each paper will have a discount registration fee at AUD 300.
