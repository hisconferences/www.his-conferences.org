HIS 2014

21-23th April 2014, ShenZhen, China

Call for Abstracts



Thank you for considering submission of an abstract at the HIS 2014 Conference. The abstracts submitted should cover all aspects of the health information sciences and the systems that support this health information management and health service delivery.

Abstracts must include a title and contact information including name, affiliation, email, and mailing address for each presenter. Abstracts must clearly describe the content of theresearch: the innovation or the motivation of original research, associated case study, discussion of experience, preliminary research data, completed research not presented previously, review of literature, etc. Abstracts may be submitted for oral or poster presentation. Accepted abstracts will not be printed in the HIS conference proceeding.

Abstracts (at least one but no more than two full pages) should be submitted by 31th January 2014 as a Word or PDF file attachment using our online form.

The official language of the Conference is English and all papers must therefore be written and presented in English.

A leading author (who will present the paper at the Conference) should be designated; each leading author may submit no more than three abstracts and will be considered as corresponding author by the Organization.
