Paper Submission

What to submit?
The HIS2018 Proceedings will be published by Springer. Papers should be submitted in LNCS (Lecture Notes in Computer Science) format as PostScript or PDF files, and may not exceed 12 pages for regular paper, industry papers up to 6 pages.

How to submit?
HIS 2018 is using the EasyChair conference management system. The submission website is at: https://easychair.org/conferences/?conf=his20181 . Please submit your research papers and follow the instructions on the Easychair web page. The submission deadline is 7 July, 2018.

Special issues
Selected papers from the conference will be invited to submit extended versions to journals: Health Information Science and Systems(Springer).

Duplicate submissions and novelty requirements
A research paper submitted to HIS 2018 cannot be under review for any other publishing forum or presentation venue; including conferences, workshops, and journals, during the time it is being considered for HIS 2018 conference. Furthermore, after you submit a research paper to HIS 2018, you have to wait for the response from HIS 2018 and only resubmit elsewhere if your paper is rejected--or withdrawn at your request--from HIS 2018. This restriction applies to identical papers as well as to papers with a substantial overlap in scientific content and results.

Every research paper submitted to HIS 2018 must present novel research not described in any prior publication. In this context, a prior publication is,

(a) a paper of four pages or more presented, or accepted for presentation, at a refereed conference or workshop with proceedings;

or

(b) an article published, or accepted for publication, in a refereed journal. If a HIS 2018 submission has overlap with a prior publication, the submission must cite the prior publication, along with all other relevant published work.

Reviewing process
Every research paper submitted to HIS 2018 will undergo a peer reviewing process. All accepted papers will be incorporated into the conference program and included in the conference proceedings published by Springer LNCS.
