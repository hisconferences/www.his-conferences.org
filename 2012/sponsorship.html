  • The 14th Asia-Pacific Web Conference (APWeb)
  • April 11-13, 2012, Kunming, China

APweb 2012 Sponsors
The 14th Asia-Pacific Web Conference is sponsored by: Victoria University, Chinese Academy of Sciences, Northeaster University, China, Kunming Univ. of Sci. & Technology.
       

Also, Apweb 2012 seeks corporate sponsors each year. The conference offers a list of possible benefits for our corporate sponsors.
Benefits for APweb 2012 Corporate Sponsors
  • Please contact the Apweb 2012 general Chair Prof. Yanchun Zhang via Yanchun.zhang@vu.edu.au and apweb2012@gmail.com about what is expected to become one of the sponsorships below.
  • Please contact the Local Arrangements Chair Guangyan Huang via abysshuang@gmail.com if you are interested in some exhibition/demo space.
    • Apweb 2012 Bronze Sponsorship
    1. Their name and logo will be published in the conference proceedings.
    2. The conference can include their product announcements (or free products if they like) in the registration package for each conference participant.
    Apweb 2012 Silver Sponsorship
    1. Their name and logo will be published in the conference proceedings.
    2. The conference can include their product announcements (or free products if they like) in the registration package for each conference participant.
    3. Their name and/or logo will appear in our advertising (such as the calls for papers of the conference).
    4. All sponsor employees will be discounted to member rates for conference registration. (Non-member rates are at least 25% higher than member rates.)
    Apweb 2012 Gold Sponsorship
    1. Their name and logo will be published in the conference proceedings.
    2. The conference can include their product announcements (or free products if they like) in the registration package for each conference participant.
    3. Their name and/or logo will appear in our advertising (such as the calls for papers of the conference).
    4. All sponsor employees will be discounted to member rates for conference registration. (Non-member rates are at least 25% higher than member rates.)
    5. The conference will invite the company to provide a special presentation such as a tutorial or banquet speech.
    Apweb 2012 Platinum Sponsorship
    1. Their name and logo will be published in the conference proceedings.
    2. The conference can include their product announcements (or free products if they like) in the registration package for each conference participant.
    3. Their name and/or logo will appear in our advertising (such as the calls for papers of the conference).
    4. All sponsor employees will be discounted to member rates for conference registration. (Non-member rates are at least 25% higher than member rates.)
    5. The conference will invite the company to provide a special presentation such as a tutorial or banquet speech.
    6. The company can organize a major social event at the conference, such as a named lunch, breakfast, or reception.
