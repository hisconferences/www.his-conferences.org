Welcome to HIS 2025

14th International Conference on Health Information Science (HIS 2025) provides a forum for disseminating and exchanging multidisciplinary research results in computer science/information technology and health science & services. HIS 2025 aims to integrate computer science/information technology with health sciences & services, embracing information science research coupled with topics related to modelling, design, development, integration, and management of health information systems and health services. The 14th International Conference on Health Information Science (HIS 2025) will co-locate with IEEE-13th International Conference on Orange Technology (ICOT) 2025.

The scope includes:

1. Medical/health/biomedicine information resources, such as patient medical records, devices and equipment, software, and tools to capture, store, retrieve, process, analyse, and optimize the use of information in the health domain.

2. Data management, data mining, and knowledge discovery (in the health domain), all of which play a key role in decision making, management of public health, examination of standards, privacy, and security issues.

3. Development of new architectures and applications for health information systems.
