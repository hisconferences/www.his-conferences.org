About HIS 2012

The 4th International Conference on Health Information Science (HIS 2015) provides a forum for disseminating and exchanging multidisciplinary research results in computer science/information technology and health science & services. HIS 2015 aims to integrate computer science/information technology with health sciences & services, embracing information science research coupled with topics related to the modelling, design, development, integration, and management of health information systems and health services. It covers all aspects of the health information sciences and the systems that support this health information management and health service delivery.
