About Universitas Kristen Maranatha

Universitas Kristen Maranatha is one of Indonesia's premier private Christian universities, located in the heart of Bandung city. Founded in 1965, the university has established itself as a leading institution for higher education in Indonesia, consistently delivering excellence in academic programs and research.

The university's modern facilities and strategic location make it an ideal venue for international conferences. With state-of-the-art conference halls, advanced technological infrastructure, and a beautiful campus environment, Maranatha provides the perfect setting for academic discourse and professional networking.
