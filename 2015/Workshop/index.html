Welcome to 4th China-Australia Database Workshop

Every year, a group of database researchers from China and Australia meet together to discuss the state of database research, its impact on practice and important new directions. The meeting of this year will be on October 19th, just before the CIKM 2015 main conference (October 20th - 23rd ), Melbourne, Australia. Big Data has now become a defining challenge of our time, and the database research community is uniquely positioned to address it, with enormous opportunities to make transformative impact. In this workshop, we will discuss the recent Beckman report and new challenges, including the diversity in the data management landscape; End-to-End Processing and Understanding of Data; Cloud Services; spatial and temporal data analytics and managing the roles of people in the data life cycle.
